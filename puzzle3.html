Target Audience Identification

Great job on solving the market segmentation challenge! Now, let's put your skills to the test by identifying the target audience for a sports and entertainment marketing campaign. Below are descriptions of different audience segments. Can you match each description with the corresponding target audience?

Segments Descriptions
1) Demographic A) This audience segment includes individuals who are passionate about outdoor activities, adventure sports, and extreme sports.
2) Psychographic B) This audience segment comprises residents of urban areas with a population of over 1 million people.
3) Geographic C) This audience segment consists of frequent concertgoers who attend live music events at least once a month.
4) Behavioral D) This audience segment consists of males and females aged 18-34, with a college education and an annual income of $50,000 or more.

Hint: Make sure all letters are lowercase.
