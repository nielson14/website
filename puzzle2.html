Market Segmentation Challenge

Congratulations on solving the first puzzle! You're one step closer to mastering the world of sports and entertainment marketing. Now, let's delve into the concept of market segmentation, a crucial aspect of any successful marketing strategy.

Below are descriptions of different types of consumer segments. Can you match each description with the corresponding term?

Term Definition
1) Demographic A) This segmentation strategy categorizes consumers based on their lifestyle, personality traits, interests, and values.
2) Psychographic B) This segmentation strategy divides the market based on characteristics such as age, gender, income, and education level.
3) Geographic C) This segmentation strategy focuses on consumer behavior, including purchasing patterns, usage rate, brand loyalty, and product benefits sought.
4) Behavioral D) This segmentation strategy divides the market based on location, such as region, country, city, or climate.
5) Market Segmentation E) Dividing a broad target market into smaller, more manageable segments based on common characteristics, needs, or behaviors.

Hint: Make sure all letters are lowercase.
